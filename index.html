Welcome to Star Wars: The Force Breaker

The Rebellions force gathered up and the attack on the galactic empire has begun. First mission to destroy the great wall.

Online Play

  • The Game can take a while for loading despite the small size and encounter some lag at the beginning.
  • Click the mouse to launch the ball projectile
  • Move the mouse right and left to control the light paddle

Unity 3D Game Engine

The Game is developed with Unity and MonoDevelop with C# as scripting Language. Unity 5.4 now features exporting to ALL platforms.
